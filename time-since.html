Occurrence trackers

Tap whenever a chore or event happens. The app calculates current, average, longest, and shortest intervals.

Session trackers

Start, pause, resume, and end sessions like fasting. Only active time affects duration stats.

Reminder logic

Mobile local notifications are scheduled from the last completion, with pause windows canceling reminders until resumed.
