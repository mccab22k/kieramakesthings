A circular 24-hour planner

Your day, in orbit.

Orbit is a visual planner built around the shape of a day: midnight at the top, noon at the bottom, and every commitment arranged around a circular 24-hour dial.

Launch Orbit View screenshots

Concept mockup only: Orbit translated into the Lustre-style cream, blush, rose, and espresso palette. The app UI is not changed.

Most planners treat time as a list.

Orbit treats time as a shape. The circular day view makes overlap, drift, empty space, and routine more visible than a stacked agenda.

Features

Circular planning

See the full day at once, from midnight through the next midnight.

Two layers

Separate longer blocks from shorter events without visual collision.

Drag scheduling

Move blocks around the dial and resize time visually.

Templates

Build reusable daily structures for common routines.

Repeating tasks

Plan routines that recur or regenerate after completion.

Month view

Zoom out from a single day into broader planning.
