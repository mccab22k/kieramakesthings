Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Orbit is designed to help users plan and organize their time. Privacy is intentionally simple: your planning information stays on your device.

Information We Collect

Orbit stores planning information locally on your device, including tasks, schedules, templates, and preferences. I do not collect this information, keep a copy of it, or transmit it to any server.

How Information Is Used

Your information is used only within the app to provide planning features. It is not sent to me or to any external server for processing, analytics, advertising, or profiling.

Data Sharing

Orbit does not sell, rent, share, or transmit personal information. Because I do not receive or keep your planning data, I cannot share it with third parties.

Data Security

Your planning information remains on your device. You are responsible for managing access to your device and any device-level backups or sync settings you choose to enable.

Your Choices

You may modify or delete your planning information where supported by the app. Removing the app or clearing app data may remove locally stored planning information, depending on your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Orbit is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated periodically.

Contact

Questions may be directed through the project's GitHub page.
